# Case Study 01 — Aligned & Healthy (control)

## Scenario
A small product team that's genuinely doing well. The team agrees the
environment is psychologically safe, communication is open, and the manager
is effective. This case is the **control** — it should produce a Low risk
score, an "Aligned" alignment band, and no fracture/split signals.

## Organisation
- **Industry:** SaaS / B2B software product
- **Team:** Onboarding & implementation squad
- **Department slug:** `onboarding`
- **N respondents:** 8 (2 senior engineers, 4 mid-level engineers, 2 junior CSMs)

## Conflict signature being tested
- High means across all scale items
- Low variance / high r_wg → **Aligned** band
- Booleans uniformly 0 (no escalation, no unsafe behaviour, no unresolved conflict)
- No split items, no subgroup detection

## Expected metric outcomes
| Signal | Expected |
|---|---|
| Risk score | 15–25 (Low) |
| Team Alignment | 80–95 (Aligned) |
| Psych Safety mean | ~8.5, r_wg ≥0.85 |
| Communication & Trust mean | ~8.3, r_wg ≥0.85 |
| Conflict Frequency mean | ~2.0, r_wg ≥0.80 |
| Management Effectiveness mean | ~8.4, r_wg ≥0.85 |
| Wellbeing & Belonging mean | ~8.5, r_wg ≥0.85 |
| Split items (BC > 0.555) | 0 |
| Subgroups | None (silhouette < 0.5) |
| Quality drops | 0 |
| AI narrative | Healthy team; document & maintain |
