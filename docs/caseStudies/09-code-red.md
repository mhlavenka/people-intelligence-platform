# Case Study 09 — Code Red (uniformly bad, escalation-ready)

## Scenario
A team where the situation is acutely bad and **everyone agrees**. Low
psychological safety, low trust, ineffective management, high tension,
multiple respondents asking for HR intervention. This is the
Critical-risk case where intervention needs to be both immediate AND
external (HR / mediation), because the in-team capacity to resolve is
exhausted.

Aligned + uniformly low is structurally different from Polarised (Case 03)
or Two Shifts (Case 04) — the team is together, but together at the
bottom. The intervention is: address the issue head-on, possibly with
external mediation, and give the team something concrete to rebuild around.

## Organisation
- **Industry:** Healthcare / community clinic
- **Team:** Front-desk admin team (after a difficult quarter)
- **Department slug:** `front-desk`
- **N respondents:** 8

## Conflict signature being tested
- All scale items LOW (1–4)
- All booleans HIGH (mostly 1s for cp03, cp08, cp12)
- r_wg high (≥0.7) — everyone agrees
- Risk score Critical
- No split items (consistent with uniform agreement on bad)
- Multiple "yes" answers on cp12 (HR intervention requested)
- AI narrative: "External intervention required; in-team resolution capacity is depleted"
